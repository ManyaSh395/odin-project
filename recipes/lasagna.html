Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground Beef(or any meet of choice)
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Steps

  1. To make lasagna, start by cooking lasagna noodles until al dente and preparing a rich meat sauce using ground beef, tomatoes, garlic, and Italian herbs.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg, salt, and pepper.
  3. In a baking dish, layer the ingredients starting with a spoonful of sauce, followed by noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and repeat the layers until the dish is full, finishing with sauce and a generous topping of cheese.
  4. Bake it in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 45 minutes until bubbly and golden, then let it rest before serving.
